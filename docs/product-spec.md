## Objective
Automatically augment personal brand operation on X and Threads.

## Persona Target
Focuses purely on AI, Capital strategies, Business judgments, and high-quality aesthetic lifestyle mapping.

## Modules Included
- Local markdown notes ingestion
- LLM powered logic ranking against persona definition
- Drafting pipelines targeted at both short-form and thread structures
- A risk Guardian module 
- Local analytics and learning feedback systems.
